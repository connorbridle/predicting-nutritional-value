Some general notes from papers:

eatwell0708:
	Sugar 
	High sugar content is more than 15g per 100g
	Low sugar content is 5g or less per 100g

	Salt
	High salt content is more than 1.5g per 100g (or 0.6g sodium)
	Low salt content is 0.3g salt or less per 100g (or 0.1 sodium)

	Saturated fat
	High sat fat content is more than 5g saturates per 100g
	Low sat fat content is 1.5g or less per 100g

	Fat
	High fat content is more than 20g per 100g
	Low fat content is 3g fat or less per 100g
